Inagh Taekwon - Do

Established in 20xx by Jamie Rigney, a 6th Degree Black Belt, the Inagh Taekwon-Do Club is a vibrant and welcoming ITF Taekwon-Do community in Clare. We offer classes for all ages and skill levels.

Our club is dedicated to fostering a supportive environment where individuals can develop discipline, strength, agility, and overall physical fitness through high-quality Taekwon-Do training.
